# Instant Pot Steel Cut Oats

### Combine in instant pot
* 80g dry steel cut oats per serving
* 240g water per serving (can replace up to 1/4 of total weight with milk to taste)

### Cook in instant pot
* Run for 4 minutes on high pressure
* Allow to keep warm for 10min

### Make tasty
* Add brown sugar, maple syrup, nuts, dried fruit, spices, etc.
* flavorful ingredients may be added before cooking, but there doesn't seem to be any benefit
